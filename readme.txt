Adds the author information block on the post page.

== Description ==
Introducing the incredible "Theme-Styled Author Information Block"! Elevate your post pages to new heights with this fully customizable block that will captivate your readers from the very first glance.

Unleash your creativity and personalize every aspect of the Author Information Block with these amazing options:

Name: The author can choose any alphabetical name, allowing for a unique and memorable identity to shine through.

Punch Line: Grab your readers' attention with a captivating line that summarizes the essence of your post or entices them to delve deeper into your content.

About Author: Tell your readers who you are and what sets you apart. Craft a compelling text that showcases your expertise, passions, and unique perspective.

Avatar: Make a lasting impression by adding a visually striking avatar that represents your personality or brand. Let your readers connect with you on a more personal level.

Top Three Social Media: Stay connected and expand your reach by attaching up to three links to your favorite social media handles. Choose from an extensive list of popular platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Skype, YouTube, WordPress, Flickr, RSS, Google Plus, Tumblr, E-mail, Buffer, Reddit, and Stumble Upon.

Imagine the possibilities! With the Theme-Styled Author Information Block, your post pages will become an immersive experience where your readers can easily connect with your content, get to know you better, and explore your online presence.

Get ready to captivate your audience like never before and make a lasting impression with the power of the Theme-Styled Author Information Block. Your journey towards engaging and influential content starts here!

== Installation ==
= Automated Installation =
1. Navigate to the "Plugin" option located within your Admin Panel.
2. Click on the "Add New" button.
3. In the search bar, enter "One Author" to locate the desired plugin.
4. Locate the "One Author" plugin in the search results and click on the "Install Now" button. Once the installation process is complete, click on "Activate" to enable the plugin's features.
* Congratulations! You have successfully installed and activated the "One Author" plugin. You are now ready to enjoy its benefits.

= Manual Installation =
1. Open your web browser and visit the GitHub repository for the "One Author" plugin by following this link: https://github.com/Ambidextrous13/one-author.git.
2. On the repository page, locate the green "Code" button and click on it. From the dropdown menu, select "Download ZIP" to initiate the download process. Wait for the download to complete.
3. Access the admin panel of your WordPress site. From the dashboard, navigate to the "Plugin" tab. Then, click on "Add New" to proceed.
4. Within the "Add Plugins" page, select the "Upload Plugin" button located at the top of the screen.
5. Click the "Choose File" button and select the ZIP file you downloaded earlier. Once selected, click on "Install Now" to begin the installation process.
6. After the installation is complete, navigate to the sub-menu under the "Plugin" tab called "Installed Plugins." Look for the "one-author" plugin in the list and click on the "Activate" button located just below its name.
* Congratulations! The "One Author" plugin is now successfully installed and activated on your WordPress site. You can now take advantage of its features and configure it according to your preferences.
